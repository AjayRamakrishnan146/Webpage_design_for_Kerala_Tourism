Experience the Best of Nature and History in Kozhikode

Kozhikode is home to several tourist attractions, each with its own unique charm. The pristine Kappad Beach, where Vasco da Gama first set foot in India, offers breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea. The serene backwaters of Kozhikode, lined with coconut palms, are perfect for a relaxing boat ride. The Beypore port, famous for its traditional shipbuilding industry, is a testament to the city's rich maritime history. The Kozhikode district is also known for its wildlife sanctuaries, such as the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary and the Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary, which provide a rare opportunity to witness the beauty of nature up close. The Thusharagiri Waterfalls, located amidst the Western Ghats, offer a stunning view of cascading waterfalls and lush greenery. For those interested in history and culture, the Kozhikode district has several museums and monuments, such as the Pazhassiraja Museum and the Kozhikode Beach Lighthouse. The city is also famous for its traditional art forms such as Theyyam and Kathakali.Kozhikode is a destination that has something to offer for everyone. With its rich culture, diverse landscapes, and warm hospitality, it is sure to leave a lasting impression on visitors.

Explore the Top Tourist Destinations in Kozhikode

Kozhikode Beach

Kozhikode Beach, also known as Calicut Beach, is a popular tourist spot located on the Malabar Coast of Kerala. The beach is a perfect place for those seeking a peaceful getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life. The beach is known for its serene atmosphere, long stretches of golden sand, and the gentle waves of the Arabian Sea. Visitors can take a leisurely stroll along the beach, enjoy the stunning sunset view, or indulge in some water sports activities like swimming and surfing. In addition to the scenic beauty, the beach also hosts an annual event called the Kozhikode Beach Festival, which attracts tourists from all over the world.

Thusharagiri Waterfalls

Nestled amidst lush greenery and surrounded by the majestic Western Ghats, Thusharagiri Waterfalls is a must-visit tourist spot in Kozhikode. The name "Thusharagiri" means "snow-capped mountains" in Malayalam, and the waterfall gets its name from the white foam that forms at the base of the falls, giving it the appearance of snow. Trekking through the dense forest to reach the waterfalls is an adventure in itself. Once you reach the falls, the serene atmosphere and the refreshing sound of cascading water is sure to leave you spellbound. The Thusharagiri Waterfalls is an ideal spot for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

Mananchira Square

Mananchira Square is a popular attraction in the heart of Kozhikode city. It is a well-maintained park and a cultural center that hosts several events and performances throughout the year. The square is surrounded by several historical and heritage buildings, making it an important landmark of the city. The musical fountain, the artificial pond, and the lush green gardens add to the charm of the place, making it a perfect spot for a leisurely stroll or a family picnic. Mananchira Square is also an important hub for shopping and dining, with several shops and restaurants located nearby. At night, the square comes alive with illuminated fountains, adding to its charm and beauty.

Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary

Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary is a serene and tranquil paradise located in the Kadalundi village of Kozhikode. The sanctuary is spread across an area of 3 sq km and is a haven for bird enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. The sanctuary is home to over 100 species of resident and migratory birds like woodpeckers, kingfishers, egrets, and herons.Apart from bird watching, visitors can also indulge in boating and fishing in the calm waters of the Kadalundi River that flows through the sanctuary. It offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life and a chance to witness the mesmerizing beauty of the avian world.The best time to visit the sanctuary is from December to April when the migratory birds arrive.
